Fighting Cat

Welcome to the fighting cat game! 💨

Will you be able to beat the game?

📜 About the game

😺 Introduction of the game

There are two cats trying to find food and avoid eating 💩 on a table clothes. They also need to beat each other to win the game.

🍔 Fight Competence

Fight Competence can be added by 2 through eating pizza, 5 by eating hamburger, and -3 by eating shit. By the way, a cat can only eat things when its mouth is open. When one cat lose all the fight competence, the game is over.

🤛 Hit Points

When two cats meet each other, the cat with lower FC lose its hit points. After 2 cats collide, they will go back to the initial position. When one cat lose all the hit points, the game will also be over.

🎮 control the cats

The game needs two players who control the two cats.

The left cat is controlled by "q,w,a,s,d"

q stands for closing and opening its mouth

Similarly, the right one is controlled by "Space and 4 arrows"

🍶 the magic bottle

A magic bottle will appear on the table clothes for 10 seconds.

If a cat gets the magic bottle, it will turn into a dog for 5 seconds.

🐶 what a dog can do

The dog has the ability to get points by eating 💩.

Therefore, players can seize the magic bottle and try to get more fight competence.
